FAQ

자주 묻는 질문

본 페이지는 초안이며, 세부 내용은 개원 후 확정됩니다.

What treatments are available?
Implants, aesthetic treatment, prosthodontics, orthodontics & Invisalign, TMJ & sleep therapy, and preventive care. After diagnosis we guide you on a treatment plan suited to your condition.
Is guidance available in other languages?
We are preparing guidance in English, Japanese and Chinese. Multilingual consultation and treatment guidance will be supported progressively from opening.
What are your hours?
Opening hours will be announced in line with the opening schedule.
What is YIDO Total Care?
A patient-experience program that runs from check-ups and scaling through prevention, gum and recovery care. Details will be announced at opening; results may vary by individual.
What is the implant experience like?
We aim for an implant experience that connects comfort-focused anesthesia, swelling care, TMJ care, and follow-up care. We first assess honestly whether placement is truly needed, and we look after recovery and maintenance afterward. Results may vary by individual condition.
How are your prosthetic (aesthetic) restorations made?
Clinicians and our aesthetic-prosthetic lab work as one team. From diagnosis to shade and shape, we aim to design and refine each patient's restoration together. Details will be shared at opening.
