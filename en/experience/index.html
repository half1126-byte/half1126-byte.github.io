03PATIENT EXPERIENCE

Every moment of your visit,
as an experience

At Seoul Yido Dental Office, your experience matters deeply. From arrival to treatment and aftercare — we design every moment with care.

“Even a scaling, like care.” We run it as a GBT oral spa — checking the surfaces together with disclosing and cleaning with Air-Flow.

What makes the experience

Signature scent

A consistent signature scent across our spaces sets a calm mood.

Try & product area

A space to try oral-care products and receive guidance in person.

Care program — YIDO Total Care

  1. 01

    GBT oral-spa scaling

    A scaling program where we check tooth-surface condition together with disclosing, then clean thoroughly using Air-Flow.

  2. 02

    Swelling care (Wolkin LDM)

    A care program that looks after the recovery after procedures such as extractions and implants.

  3. 03

    Oral probiotics guidance

    We guide you on oral probiotics that support everyday oral care.

  4. 04

    Preventive care

    We manage oral health together through regular check-ups and an individual care plan.

  5. 05

    Oral-care pouch provided

    Program participants receive tailored oral-care items in a pouch.

※ Results may vary depending on each person's oral condition.
