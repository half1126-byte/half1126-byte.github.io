01ABOUT

The meaning behind the name,
YIDO

“Care that walks the right path.”

YIDO (履道) means “to follow the right path.” It joins the characters 履 (to tread) and 道 (the way) — a commitment to a principled, right process of care. In English, YIDO can also be read as ‘Your Ideal Dental Office.’

At Seoul YIDO Dental, clinical skill, space, hospitality and care programs are meant to flow together as one experience. Even in familiar visits like a scaling or a check-up, we want you to feel the care that goes into it.

  • 01Devotion to care

    We study many cases to keep improving how we treat.

  • 02The fundamentals

    Accurate diagnosis and treatment — the basics of care, first.

  • 03Empathy that listens

    An environment and welcome that get better with every visit.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What treatments are available?
Implants, aesthetic treatment, prosthodontics, orthodontics & Invisalign, TMJ & sleep therapy, and preventive care. After diagnosis we guide you on a treatment plan suited to your condition.
Is care available in other languages?
Clinicians who can communicate in English, Japanese, Chinese and German support your care. We assist booking, consultation and treatment guidance in multiple languages.
What are your hours?
Weekdays 09:30–18:30 (lunch 12:30–14:00), evening care until 20:30 on Mon & Thu, Saturday 09:30–14:30 (no lunch break); closed Sundays and public holidays. Subject to change with the opening schedule.
What is YIDO Total Care?
A patient-experience program that runs from check-ups and scaling through prevention, gum and recovery care. It includes GBT scaling, swelling care, oral-probiotics guidance and preventive care; results may vary by individual.
